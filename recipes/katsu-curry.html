Chicken Katsu Curry

Description

This classic japanese recipe combines the comfort of rice and curry with a juicy, crispy piece of chicken katsu. These 3 elements combine to give an incredible taste and a feeling of satisfaction from every bite.

Ingredients

  • Japanese curry roux
  • Potato
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Rice
  • Chicken cutlet

Steps

  1. Chop the potato, onion and carrot into uniform pieces
  2. In a pot, sautee the vegetables in a pot for 5 minutes
  3. Add water to the pot until the vegetables are covered
  4. Bring the vegetables to a boil and wait until the potato is easily skewered
  5. Add the curry roux to the pot and mix
  6. When the curry is thick enough, turn off the heat
  7. In a pan, fry the chicken cutlet until it is crispy on both sides
  8. Cut the chicken into thick strips
  9. Serve the rice, curry and chicken on a plate
