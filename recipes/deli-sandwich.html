Italian Deli Sandwich

Description

A sandwich loaded with salami, ham, tomato and cheese. Perfect for lunch on a hot day or for picnics.

Ingredients

  • Long bread roll
  • Sliced salami
  • Sliced ham
  • Sliced cheese
  • Sliced tomato
  • Lettuce leafs
  • Pickles
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Cut the bread roll in half length-wise
  2. Smear both sides with mayonnaise
  3. Cover one half with the sliced tomatos
  4. Add pickles on top of the tomatos
  5. Layer some lettuce on top of the pickles
  6. Fold the salami in half and place the pieces on top of the pickles
  7. Do the same with the ham
  8. Top it off with the sliced cheese
  9. Put the other half of the bread roll back on top
  10. Cut the sandwich in half and enjoy!
