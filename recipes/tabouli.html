Tabouli

Description

The pinnacle of all salads, Tabouli is the perfect combination of finely chopped vegetables with acidic and salty flavours.

Ingredients

  • Parsely
  • Tomato
  • Bulgur
  • Green onion
  • lemon
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Soak the bulgur in water
  2. Finely chop the parsely into small pieces
  3. Dice the tomato into small cubes
  4. Chop the green onion into small pieces
  5. Combine the onion, tomato and parsely in a bowl
  6. Once the bulgur is soft, drain the water from it and squeeze any excess water out of it, then add it to the bowl
  7. Add salt, olive oil and the juice of the lemon
  8. Mix thorougly and serve
